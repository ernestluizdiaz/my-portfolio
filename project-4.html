Project Overview

This project is an inventory management system for a retail shop specializing in branded shoes like Nike, Adidas, and Puma. It tracks incoming and outgoing products, monitors stock levels, and generates reports on sales, income, and profit to ensure smooth daily operations.

The system features an intuitive interface for managing product categories, updating stock quantities, and reviewing sales history. By automating inventory tracking, it minimizes errors, supports informed decision-making, and helps maintain optimal stock to meet customer demand efficiently.

With built-in analytics and reporting, the platform allows management to monitor performance trends, evaluate profitability, and plan future inventory purchases. Overall, it streamlines retail operations, saves time, and enhances productivity.
